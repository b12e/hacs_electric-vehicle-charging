# Electric Vehicle Charging Card

A beautiful and informative custom card for Home Assistant to display your electric vehicle's charging progress.

## Features

- **Visual charging progress bar** with current and target kWh
- **Real-time charging metrics** (Watts, Volts, Amperage)
- **Animated charging indicator** that speeds up/slows down based on charging rate
- **Fully configurable** - set your battery capacity and connect your own entities
- **Responsive design** that works on mobile and desktop

## Configuration

Add the card to your dashboard and configure it with your EV's entities.

See the [README](https://github.com/b12e/hacs_electric-vehicle-charging) for detailed configuration options.
